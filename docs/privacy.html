Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how TacticsMaster ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our desktop application.

Information We Collect

Data from Riot Games API

When you use TacticsMaster, we access the following data through the official Riot Games API:

  • Summoner Information: Your summoner name, profile icon, and summoner level
  • Ranked Data: Your current rank, LP, wins, and losses in TFT
  • Match History: Your recent TFT matches, including compositions played, placement, and augments selected
Important: We only access data for the account currently logged into the TFT client. We do not access data from other players without their consent.

How We Use Your Data

  • Display your personal statistics within the application
  • Provide personalized composition and augment recommendations
  • Train machine learning models using aggregated, anonymized match data
  • Improve our recommendation algorithms

Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: Your personal data is stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not upload or store your personal data on external servers
  • Aggregated Data: Match data used for ML training is anonymized and contains no personally identifiable information

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real name or email address
  • Payment or financial information
  • IP address or location data
  • Data from other players
  • Chat logs or private messages

Third-Party Services

TacticsMaster uses the official Riot Games API to access game data. Your use of TacticsMaster is also subject to Riot Games' Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Personal data is stored locally on your device and persists until you uninstall the application or manually clear the app data. We do not retain any personal data on our servers.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data by viewing it within the application
  • Delete your data by uninstalling the application
  • Opt out of data collection by not using the application

Children's Privacy

TacticsMaster is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
